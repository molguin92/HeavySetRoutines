Based on the book by Dr. Randall Strossen ("Super Squats: How To Gain 30 Pounds of Muscle in 6 Weeks") and Alexander Bromley's modified version of the program (https://empire-barbell.com/2021/07/23/super-squats-review-of-the-legendary-20-rep-squat-program/).

3 workouts/week. Modified for some variations on the non-squat exercises, feel free to repeat any one single day 3x week, or exchange exercises.

---

## Super Squats A
A. Seated Behind-the-Neck Press, 3 sets, 12 reps
B. Dip, 3 sets, 12 reps
C. Reverse Grip Lat Pulldown, 3 sets, 12 reps
D. Squat, 1 set, 20 reps
E. Pullover, 1 set, 20 reps
F. RDL, 1 set, 15 reps
G. Pullover, 1 set, 20 reps
H. Calf Raise, 1 set, 20 reps

## Super Squats B
A. Overhead Press, 3 sets, 12 reps
B. Bench Press, 3 sets, 12 reps
C. Barbell Row, 3 sets, 12 reps
D. Squat, 1 set, 20 reps
E. Pullover, 1 set, 20 reps
F. Straight Legged Deadlift, 1 set, 15 reps
G. Pullover, 1 set, 20 reps
H. Deadbug, 2 sets, 20 reps

## Super Squats C
A. Seated Behind-the-Neck Press, 3 sets, 12 reps
B. Dip, 3 sets, 12 reps
C. Reverse Grip Lat Pulldown, 3 sets, 12 reps
D. Squat, 1 set, 20 reps
E. Pullover, 1 set, 20 reps
